# AI Academic Performance Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of how Generative AI (GenAI) tools influence students' academic journeys. By analyzing data from **50K students**, the project explores the correlation between AI tool adoption, academic performance (GPA), and student anxiety levels across various majors and academic years.

## 🔑 Comprehensive Insights & Dashboard Contents
* **KPI Cards (Overview):** Displays the total student population (**50K**) alongside a quick comparative view of average GPAs across key disciplines (**STEM GPA: 3.36**, **Medical GPA: 3.35**, **Humanities GPA: 3.35**, and **Business GPA: 3.34**).
* **AI Engagement & GPA Overview (Matrix Table):** A detailed matrix breaking down each Major Category by its **Average Weekly GenAI Hours**, **Post Semester GPA**, and **Tool Diversity count** (highlighting STEM with the highest engagement of 10.49 hours and 43,910 tool diversity).
* **AI Primary Use Cases (Bar Chart):** Tracks what students use AI for, revealing **Debugging/Troubleshooting** as the top use case, followed closely by Copywriting/Drafting and Ideation.
* **AI Adoption by Year of Study (Line Chart):** Illustrates the growth curve of AI adoption across academic levels, showing that **Junior** and **Freshman** students have the highest adoption rates, while Graduate students show the lowest.
* **AI Usage Anxiety Levels (Histogram/Bar Chart):** Analyzes the psychological impact, showing that most students experience moderate anxiety levels (scaled between 3 and 5) regarding AI integration.
* **AI Subscription Plans (Donut Chart):** Breaks down the distribution of student enrollment in different AI subscription tiers.
* **Interactive Slicers:** Dynamic sidebar filters allowing users to slice the entire dashboard by **Major Category** and **Year of Study** seamlessly.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
* **Power BI Desktop:** Advanced data visualization, interactive dashboard architecture, and custom layout design.
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Utilized for calculating key metrics and time-study insights.
* **Microsoft Excel:** Data transformation, cleaning, and initial formatting.
